Our Mission

The Berea Economics & Data Science Association (BEDSA) is a student-led organization at Berea College that explores the powerful connection between economics and data. We provide a community where students can learn, collaborate, and apply data-driven approaches to real-world issues in economics, policy, and society.

Through workshops, guest talks, events, and collaborative projects, BEDSA helps members strengthen their academic and professional skills while building meaningful connections with peers, faculty, and industry professionals.

Whether you are pursuing a career in economics, data science, or simply curious about how data shapes our world, BEDSA is a place to grow, share ideas, and make an impact.
